Our Story

Built on immigrant roots, hands-on work, and a commitment to protecting local businesses.

HiohSec started with my family's story. My parents came to Ohio as immigrants and spent decades working in greenhouses and nurseries. They kept operations running through long days, tough seasons, and constant problem-solving. That work ethic shaped how I see responsibility and what it means to support a business.

I grew up in that environment as a first-generation American. HiohSec began with the goal of helping the industry that supported my family, but it became clear that many small businesses in Ohio face the same security gaps. Today, the company supports any local business that needs practical, affordable cybersecurity while still honoring the roots that shaped its mission.

HiohSec is based in Cleveland and in its first year. The mission is simple: protect small businesses with clear, reliable, hands-on support.
